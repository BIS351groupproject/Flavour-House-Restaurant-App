About Us

‹

The Beginning

Flavour House was founded in Texas by John Phillips and has been serving traditional American comfort food for generations. We pride ourselves on high quality dishes made with fresh ingredients. Today, we have branches across the United States and internationally in London, Paris and Berlin, with plans to expand into the Middle East.

Better Service, Same Great Taste!

Due to our popularity, we often deal with high customer traffic, which can lead to overcrowding. To address this problem we developed this web application to help you order your favorite food online from the comfort of your homes.
