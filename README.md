## Project idea
Flavor House is a restaurant web application that our team made, it is a prototype designed to represent a traditional American restaurant experience.

The restaurant was founded in Texas by John Phillips and serves classic American comfort food such as steaks, mashed potatoes and apple pie.

The goal of our application is to allow customers to easily search for the restaueant online whether its for viewing the menu, or placing food orders, or reading customers reviews.

## Features
Our website contains the nine pages:
- Login Page
- Home Page
- Profile Page
- Menu Page
- Cart Page
- Checkout Page
- About Us Page
- Contact Us Page
- Reviews Page

These pages allow users to navigate the restaurant website freely and to also learn about the restaurant and its history, seeing how many branches they have across the USA and Europe.
